ChefInCasa

CHEFINCASA offers a range of typical Italian dishes from different regions of Italy. Every recipe is packaged in a practical kit that contains all the ingredients perfectly measured and carefully selected. Each ingredient is certified organic and meticulously prepared. Each kit makes 2-3 tasty portions and clearly details the preparation and techniques required to make a great dish. The aim is to allow everyone, everywhere, the ability to cook authentic pasta dishes using traditional recipes in a quick and simple way.
