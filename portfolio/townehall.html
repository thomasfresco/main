TowneHall

Located in the heart of West Island, Towne Hall offers a seasonally inspired cuisine with European influences. The atmosphere is welcoming, much like a neighborhood classic, with a sophisticated yet casual elegance. With its grand entrance, opulent crystal chandeliers, an expansive bar area, grand booths, open kitchen and wood-burning oven, Towne Hall embraces its European roots while delivering a modern and fresh dining experience. The menu has a wide selection of wood-fired pizzas, pastas, sandwiches, salads and a daily home-style cooked meal. Towne Hall also features an adventurous wine list, representing both boutique and well-known vineyards.
